DRLPEB

Deep Reinforcement Learning empowered Private Ethereum Blockchain

The program combines deep reinforcement learning (especially PPO & DDQN) with private Ethereum blockchains to optimize performance from point view of TPS and tx_delay. This innovative approach enhances transaction efficiency and smart contract execution, paving the way for advanced, intelligent blockchain solutions.
